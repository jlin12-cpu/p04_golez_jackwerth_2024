# Chart: Treasury Rates Over Time
Time series of 1-month T-bill, 2-year, and 10-year Treasury rates. Shows the decline in interest rates from 1990s levels (~6%) to near-zero during 2008 crisis and COVID, followed by rapid increases in 2022-2024.
